Pricing Model (IMPORTANT):
- Prices are calculated per meter (per "متر طولي").
- Each product has a "price_per_meter" in SAR.
- In product details and cart, the user selects "عدد الأمتار" (meters) as a numeric input (decimal allowed, e.g., 2.5).
- Total item price = price_per_meter * meters.
- Cart subtotal is the sum of all item totals.
- If a product is custom priced, show "حسب الطلب" and disable direct checkout price, allowing "طلب عرض سعر" flow.
